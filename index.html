Lagos

Lagos is the largest city in Nigeria and one of the fastest-growing urban centers in the world. Located along the Atlantic coast, it serves as the country’s commercial and economic hub, with a vibrant mix of industries, trade, and culture. Known for its lively markets, busy ports, and dynamic nightlife, Lagos is also home to beautiful beaches, cultural landmarks, and a rich diversity of people. Despite challenges like traffic congestion and rapid urbanization, the city continues to thrive as a center of innovation, entertainment, and opportunity in West Africa.
